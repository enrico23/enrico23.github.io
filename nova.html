Nova — Platform Launch

Nova is an SMB PC repair service. I was engaged to design and deliver the platform and operational foundation enabling the business’ digital presence, service intake, and future operational tooling.

The first production release establishes a secure, automated, low-overhead platform baseline designed to support iterative capability expansion as the business scales.

Ownership Scope

  • Platform architecture and hosting strategy
  • Deployment automation and release workflows
  • Production infrastructure and security baseline
  • Domain, communication, and service presence enablement
  • Forward roadmap definition for operational systems

Platform Architecture

  • Designed and deployed the web platform foundation on Azure Static Web Apps
  • Selected hosting model to balance production reliability with minimal operational overhead
  • Structured the solution to support phased capability rollout

Delivery & Release Engineering

  • Implemented GitHub-driven CI/CD pipelines
  • Established automated build and deployment workflows
  • Enabled repeatable, low-risk production releases
  • Standardised deployment processes to support rapid iteration

Infrastructure & Security Baseline

  • Configured production domain routing and DNS architecture
  • Enabled HTTPS and TLS encryption across the public platform surface
  • Provisioned operational email infrastructure for customer communications

Service Presence Enablement

  • Delivered the initial public landing experience
  • Established digital intake and contact pathways
  • Launched supporting social presence to support customer acquisition

Engineering Characteristics

  • Low-ops hosting and cost-efficient architecture
  • Automated deployment and release workflows
  • Secure production baseline from first release
  • Designed for incremental platform expansion

Current Status

The platform foundation is live in production and actively supporting the business' initial service operations.

Forward Roadmap
